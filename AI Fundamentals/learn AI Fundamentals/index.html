    AI Fundamentals

    Understanding Artificial Intelligence for Finance Professionals

    Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming how finance professionals analyse businesses, build financial models, conduct research, and make decisions.

    Rather than viewing AI as a replacement for finance expertise, AI Finance Lab views AI as a powerful collaborator.

    The professionals who thrive in the coming decade will combine:

    • Strong finance fundamentals
    • Business judgement
    • AI fluency

    What is Artificial Intelligence?

    Artificial Intelligence refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that traditionally require human intelligence.

    Examples include:

    • Understanding language
    • Recognising patterns
    • Generating text
    • Writing code
    • Summarising documents
    • Answering questions

    Modern AI systems are particularly effective at processing large amounts of information quickly.

    What is Generative AI?

    Generative AI creates new content based on patterns learned from existing data.

    In the financial context, it can generate:

    • Reports
    • Summaries
    • Financial analysis
    • Code
    • Presentations
    • Spreadsheets
    • Charts
    • Explanations

    For finance professionals, Generative AI acts like a highly knowledgeable research assistant—not a replacement for professional judgement.

    How Finance Professionals Use AI

    AI is already helping professionals:

    Research

    • Summarise annual reports
    • Compare competitors
    • Review earnings calls

    Financial Analysis

    • Explain financial statements
    • Identify trends
    • Highlight unusual movements

    Financial Modelling

    • Suggest Excel formulas
    • Build simple calculations
    • Explain modelling logic
    • Generate documentation

    Productivity

    • Draft emails
    • Create presentations
    • Summarise meetings
    • Generate reports

    What AI Does Well

    AI excels at:

    • Processing information quickly
    • Explaining concepts
    • Generating first drafts
    • Finding patterns
    • Organising information
    • Automating repetitive tasks

    What AI Does Poorly

    AI may:

    • Hallucinate facts
    • Misinterpret context
    • Produce incorrect calculations
    • Use outdated information
    • Sound confident while being wrong

    Understanding these limitations is essential for every finance professional.

    Human Judgement Still Matters

    Finance requires:

    • Critical thinking
    • Commercial judgement
    • Ethical decision-making
    • Risk assessment
    • Context

    These remain human responsibilities.

    The AI Finance Mindset

    Think of AI as:

    An intelligent junior analyst that works incredibly fast—but whose work must always be reviewed.

    Learning Outcomes

    After completing this lesson, you should understand:

    • What AI is
    • How Generative AI works
    • Where AI fits into finance
    • AI's strengths and limitations
    • Why finance expertise remains essential
