# Contribute to AI Finance Lab

## Building the future of finance education together

AI Finance Lab is built on a simple belief:

> Knowledge becomes more valuable when it is shared.

The future of finance will be shaped by professionals who understand both financial fundamentals and emerging technologies.

This initiative welcomes contributions from people who want to help make practical AI-enabled finance education more accessible.

# Contribution Principles

All contributions should follow these principles:

## Practical

Focus on real-world applications rather than theory alone.

## Accurate

Content should maintain professional finance standards.

## Accessible

Resources should help learners at different stages.

## Educational

The goal is knowledge sharing, not promotion.

---

# Editorial Standards

AI Finance Lab follows an editorial review process.

Contributions may be reviewed for:

- Accuracy
- Relevance
- Clarity
- Educational value

AI Finance Lab reserves the right to edit, improve, or organize contributions to maintain consistency and quality.
