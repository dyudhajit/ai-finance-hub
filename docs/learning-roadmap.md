# AI Finance Lab Learning Roadmap

## Becoming an AI-Enabled Finance Professional

The future finance professional will not be defined by knowing every AI tool.

They will be defined by understanding:

- Finance fundamentals
- Business decision-making
- Data and technology
- How to apply AI effectively

This roadmap provides a structured learning journey from finance foundations to advanced AI-enabled workflows.

---

# Learning Philosophy

AI Finance Lab follows three principles:

## 1. Fundamentals First

Technology is powerful only when combined with strong domain expertise.

## 2. Practical Application

Every concept should connect to a real finance workflow.

## 3. Continuous Learning

AI is evolving rapidly. Finance professionals must continuously adapt.
