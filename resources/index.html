    Curated Resources for AI-Enabled Finance Professionals

    There has never been a better time to learn finance.

    There has also never been a more overwhelming number of tools, courses, books, and AI applications.

    The purpose of this page is to curate high-quality resources that complement the learning materials in AI Finance Lab.

    The focus is always on practical learning rather than collecting the largest list of links.

    Learning Roadmap

    If you are new to finance, we recommend following this order:

    1. Accounting Fundamentals
    2. Financial Statements
    3. Corporate Finance
    4. Financial Modelling
    5. Valuation
    6. Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals
    7. AI Applications in Finance

    • pandas

    • yfinance

    Annual Reports

    Reading annual reports is one of the fastest ways to improve business understanding.

    Recommended companies:

    • Berkshire Hathaway
    • Apple
    • Microsoft
    • Alphabet
    • Amazon
    • Breville Group

    Suggested exercise:

    Read one annual report every month and answer:

    • How does the company make money?
    • What are the major risks?
    • Where is cash generated?
    • What drives growth?
    • What concerns management?
